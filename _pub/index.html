I am a contradance caller and choreographer based in New York City. I’ve been calling in and around the Northeast for the past four years, at venues including the NEFFA festival, the Concord Scout House, BIDA, Glen Echo, CDNY, Brooklyn Contra, Baltimore MD, and Bennington VT, with bands like The Free Raisins, The Russet Trio, Coracree, and Black River Ironworks. (References available on request.)

I also have experience as a dance organizer: during undergrad, I ran the monthly Williams College dance for three years; I currently serve on CDNY’s Outreach and Retention Committee; and I organize contradance flash mobs sometimes.

Read on for media samples, check out my upcoming and past gigs, or look at some dances I’ve written or tunes I’ve written.
